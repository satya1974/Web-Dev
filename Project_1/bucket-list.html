My Nature-Inspired Bucket List

Dreams to Conquer

  1. ⛰️ Kedarnath

    Experience the spiritual energy of the Himalayas on a soul-refreshing trek to Kedarnath temple.

  2. 🏍️ Ladakh Bike Riding

    Ride through the world’s highest motorable roads, surrounded by snow-capped peaks and rugged deserts.

  3. 🏔️ Mount Everest

    Witness the grandeur of the highest point on Earth — even just getting close is a victory!

  4. 🌄 Araku Valley

    Explored the lush green coffee plantations and tribal culture nestled in the Eastern Ghats.

  5. 🏖️ Goa

    Enjoyed the waves, vibrant beaches, and electrifying nightlife in India’s beach paradise.

  6. 🏯 Hari Hara Fort

    Conquered the historical trek to the top of this lesser-known but thrilling fort in Maharashtra.

  7. 🏜️ Sahara Desert

    Dreaming of camel rides and endless dunes under the magical African sunset sky.

  8. 🗼 Going to Paris

    Wander through the romantic streets of Paris, visit the Eiffel Tower, and indulge in authentic French cuisine.
